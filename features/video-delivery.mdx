After you submit your custom intro request, your video is generated and delivered directly to your email. This page explains the delivery process, what to expect, and how to troubleshoot any issues.

## How delivery works

Your custom Stranger Things intro is delivered through a simple, automated email process:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Fill out all required fields on the [Stranger Things Intro Creator](https://gostargazer.com/stranger-things/) form, including your email address and chosen resolution.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Video generation begins">
    Your custom intro enters the rendering queue. The server processes your text customizations and generates your video at the selected resolution.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Email is sent">
    Once rendering is complete, an email is automatically sent to the address you provided with your custom intro attached or linked.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download and enjoy">
    Open the email, download your video, and start using your custom Stranger Things intro.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Delivery timeline

Understand when to expect your custom intro based on various factors:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Typical timeline">
    **Standard delivery:** Shortly after submission
    
    You'll receive your custom intro via email shortly after submitting your request:
    
    - Higher resolutions (4K) generally take longer to process than lower resolutions
    - Delivery times may vary based on server load and queue length
    - Most videos are delivered within minutes to an hour
    
    <Note>
      Actual delivery times depend on current demand and the resolution you selected.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Peak times">
    During high-traffic periods, delivery may take longer:
    
    **Factors that affect delivery speed:**
    - Time of day (peak hours vs. off-peak)
    - Number of requests in queue
    - Resolution selected (4K takes longest)
    - Server maintenance windows
    
    <Tip>
      If you need your intro by a specific deadline, submit your request well in advance to account for potential delays.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="What's happening">
    Behind the scenes, your intro goes through processing:
    
    1. Your request joins the processing queue
    2. Your custom text is formatted and rendered
    3. The intro video is generated at your chosen resolution
    4. The completed video is sent to your email address
    
    Higher resolutions require more processing time than lower resolutions.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### File delivery method

How you receive your video depends on its size:

**Direct attachment (SD and most HD videos):**
- Video file attached directly to email
- Download by saving the attachment
- May be blocked by some email providers with strict attachment limits

**Download link (large HD and 4K videos):**
- Email contains a secure download link
- Click the link to download your video
- Link remains active for a limited time

<Tip>
  Download your video promptly after receiving the email. If using a download link, it may expire after a certain period.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting delivery issues

If you don't receive your custom intro, try these steps:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check your spam/junk folder" icon="filter">
    Automated emails sometimes get filtered as spam:
    
    **Steps:**
    1. Open your email spam or junk folder
    2. Look for an email about your Stranger Things intro
    3. Mark it as "Not Spam" if found
    4. Check your inbox settings to allow future emails
    
    <Tip>
      Add the sender to your contacts or safe senders list to prevent future emails from being filtered.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Verify your email address" icon="envelope-circle-check">
    Confirm you entered your email correctly:
    
    **Common mistakes:**
    - Typos in the email address
    - Missing @ symbol or domain
    - Wrong domain extension (.com vs .net)
    - Extra spaces before or after
    
    If you made an error, you'll need to submit a new request with the correct email address.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Check email provider limits" icon="ban">
    Some email providers have restrictions:
    
    **Issues to check:**
    - Maximum attachment size limits (4K videos may exceed limits)
    - Security settings blocking video files
    - Inbox storage quota full
    - Firewall or corporate email restrictions
    
    <Warning>
      If your email provider blocks large attachments, the email may be rejected. Try using a different email service for 4K videos.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wait longer" icon="clock">
    Sometimes delivery takes longer than expected:
    
    - High server load during peak times
    - Large queue of pending requests
    - Complex rendering for 4K videos
    
    If it's been less than an hour, wait a bit longer before troubleshooting further.
    
    <Note>
      4K videos can take up to 30 minutes or more during busy periods. Be patient before assuming there's a problem.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Try a different email address" icon="envelope">
    If all else fails, submit a new request:
    
    1. Use a different email provider (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
    2. Ensure the email address is correct
    3. Submit a new request with the same customizations
    
    <Tip>
      Gmail and other major providers generally have high attachment limits and reliable delivery.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use a temporary email address?">
    While technically possible, it's not recommended:
    
    - Temporary email services may have low attachment limits
    - You might lose access before delivery completes
    - Some services block automated emails
    
    Use a permanent email address you control.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Will I receive a confirmation email immediately?">
    You typically won't receive an immediate confirmation after submitting your request. The only email you'll receive is the delivery email with your completed intro.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I redownload if I lose the file?">
    The delivery email remains in your inbox, so you can download from the original email if needed. However, download links may expire after a certain period, so save the video file itself as a backup.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if the video file is corrupted?">
    If your downloaded video won't play or appears corrupted:
    
    1. Try downloading again from the email
    2. Try a different video player
    3. Check if the download completed fully
    4. Submit a new request if the problem persists
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
