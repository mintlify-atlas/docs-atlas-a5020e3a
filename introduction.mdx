## What is the Stranger Things Intro Creator?

The Stranger Things Intro Creator is a SaaS web application that generates custom videos recreating the iconic Stranger Things opening sequence with your personalized text. Simply fill out a web form with your desired text, select your preferred video quality, and receive a professionally rendered video via email.
