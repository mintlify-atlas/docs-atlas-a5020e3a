## Create your first intro

Follow these simple steps to generate your personalized Stranger Things intro video.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the creator">
    Go to [gostargazer.com/stranger-things](https://gostargazer.com/stranger-things/) to access the intro creator form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in your custom text">
    Complete the text fields with your personalized content:
    
    - **Start Text** - The opening text that appears before the main title
    - **Title Row 1** - The first line of your main title
    - **Title Row 2** - The second line of your main title
    - **End Text** - The closing text that appears after the main title
    
    <Tip>
      Keep your text concise for the best visual effect. The iconic Stranger Things font works best with shorter phrases.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Provide a valid email address where you'd like to receive your custom video.
    
    <Note>
      Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select your resolution">
    Choose your preferred video quality:
    
    - **SD** - Standard definition, smaller file size
    - **HD** - High definition, balanced quality and size
    - **4K** - Ultra high definition, maximum quality
    
    <Tip>
      Choose HD or 4K for professional projects, or SD for quick previews and social media posts.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Click the submit button to send your request for processing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next?

After you submit your request:

1. Your custom intro enters our rendering queue
2. Our system generates your video with the exact text and resolution you specified
3. You receive an email with your custom Stranger Things intro video

<Note>
  Video delivery times may vary depending on the selected resolution and current queue length. Higher resolutions (4K) require more processing time than standard definition.
</Note>
