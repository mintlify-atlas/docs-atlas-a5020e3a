Find answers to the most common questions about creating your custom Stranger Things intro videos.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to receive my video?">
    You'll receive your custom Stranger Things intro video via email shortly after submitting your request. The processing time is typically just a few minutes, though it may vary depending on the resolution you selected and current server load.

    Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox right away.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What resolutions are available?">
    You can choose from three resolution options when creating your video:

    - **SD (Standard Definition)** - Fastest processing, smaller file size
    - **HD (High Definition)** - Balanced quality and file size
    - **4K (Ultra High Definition)** - Highest quality, larger file size

    Select the resolution that best fits your intended use case. For social media sharing, HD is usually sufficient, while 4K is ideal for high-quality productions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create multiple videos?">
    Yes! You can create as many custom Stranger Things intro videos as you'd like. Each submission generates a new video with your specified text, so feel free to experiment with different combinations and create multiple versions.

    This is perfect for creating different videos for various occasions, friends, or creative projects.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What email address should I use?">
    Use a valid email address that you check regularly, as this is where your completed video will be delivered. The email address field is required when submitting your request.

    <Tip>
      Make sure the email address is typed correctly to avoid delivery issues. Double-check for typos before submitting.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is this an official Stranger Things product?">
    No, the Stranger Things Intro Creator is a fan-made tool created by [Stargazer](https://gostargazer.com/stranger-things/). It's not officially affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Netflix or the Stranger Things series creators.

    This is a creative tribute to the iconic series, allowing fans to personalize the memorable opening sequence.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use this commercially?">
    The Stranger Things Intro Creator is intended for personal, non-commercial use only. The Stranger Things brand, logo, and opening sequence design are trademarks owned by Netflix and the series creators.

    <Note>
      If you're planning to use your custom intro for commercial purposes, you should seek appropriate permissions from the rights holders.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I don't receive my video?">
    If you don't receive your video within a reasonable timeframe:

    1. **Check your spam/junk folder** - Automated emails sometimes get filtered
    2. **Verify your email address** - Make sure you entered it correctly
    3. **Wait a bit longer** - 4K videos may take more time to process
    4. **Try again** - Submit a new request if the first one doesn't arrive

    If problems persist, the issue may be temporary due to server load or maintenance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I preview before submitting?">
    Currently, there's no preview feature available before you submit your request. However, the form is straightforward with fields for your text customization:

    - Start Text
    - Title Row 1
    - Title Row 2  
    - End Text

    You can visualize how your text will appear by watching the [demo video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oItkT0GOT2k) or viewing the screenshots to understand the layout. Since you can create multiple videos, you can always refine your text and submit again if needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
