---
title: Creating your intro
description: A complete step-by-step guide to creating your custom Stranger Things intro video
---

Follow this guide to create your own personalized Stranger Things intro video that captures the iconic aesthetic of the show's opening sequence.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the creator">
    Go to [https://gostargazer.com/stranger-things/](https://gostargazer.com/stranger-things/) to access the Stranger Things Intro Creator.
    
    You'll see a form with several text fields and options for customizing your intro video.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the start text">
    The **Start Text** field sets the opening text that appears before the main title sequence. This text creates atmosphere and builds anticipation.
    
    **Examples:**
    - "A Netflix Original Series"
    - "Created by the Duffer Brothers"
    - "Somewhere in Hawkins..."
    - "The following is based on true events"
    
    <Tip>
      Keep this text short and mysterious to match the show's tone. Think about what would appear at the beginning of an episode.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in title row 1">
    **Title Row 1** is the first line of your main title. This typically contains the primary subject or name.
    
    **Examples:**
    - "STRANGER"
    - Your name ("SARAH")
    - An event ("BIRTHDAY")
    - A project name ("PROJECT")
    
    <Note>
      The text will be rendered in the iconic Stranger Things font with the characteristic red glow effect.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in title row 2">
    **Title Row 2** is the second line of your main title, completing the phrase or name.
    
    **Examples:**
    - "THINGS" (to match row 1: "STRANGER")
    - Last name ("HENDERSON")
    - "PARTY" (to match row 1: "BIRTHDAY")
    - "UPSIDE DOWN"
    
    <Tip>
      The two title rows work together to create impact. Consider how they read as a complete phrase.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add end text">
    The **End Text** field determines what appears after the main title sequence as the intro concludes.
    
    **Examples:**
    - "Episode 1: The Vanishing"
    - "Chapter One"
    - "Happy 16th Birthday!"
    - "A Fan Creation"
    - Leave it blank for a clean finish
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Provide a valid email address where you'll receive your custom intro video.
    
    <Warning>
      Double-check your email address for typos. The video will be sent to this address once processing is complete.
    </Warning>
    
    Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the video within the expected timeframe.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your resolution">
    Choose the video resolution that best fits your needs:
    
    - **SD (Standard Definition)**: Faster processing, smaller file size, suitable for quick previews or social media
    - **HD (High Definition)**: Balanced quality and file size, great for most uses including YouTube and presentations
    - **4K (Ultra High Definition)**: Maximum quality with larger file size, ideal for professional projects or large screens
    
    <Note>
      Higher resolutions will take longer to process but provide better visual quality. Choose based on where you plan to use the video.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Once you've filled in all the fields and selected your preferred resolution, click the submit button to generate your custom intro.
    
    The form will process your request and begin rendering your personalized Stranger Things intro video.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for your video">
    After submission, your video will be processed and sent to the email address you provided.
    
    **What to expect:**
    - Processing time varies based on the selected resolution
    - SD videos are typically delivered fastest
    - HD and 4K videos may take longer due to rendering requirements
    - You'll receive an email with your custom intro video attached or linked
    
    <Tip>
      Check your spam or junk folder if the email doesn't arrive in your inbox within the expected time.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

Once you receive your video, you can:
- Download it to your device
- Share it on social media platforms
- Use it in video projects or presentations
- Show it to fellow Stranger Things fans
- Create multiple versions with different text combinations

<Note>
  Feel free to experiment with different text combinations to find the perfect intro for your needs. Each submission generates a unique video based on your specifications.
</Note>
