## Text combinations that work well

The creator has four customizable text fields. Here are some effective ways to use them:

### Classic format
- **Start Text:** "Created by"
- **Title Row 1:** "Your Name"
- **Title Row 2:** "Productions"
- **End Text:** "A Fan Creation"

### Event celebration
- **Start Text:** "Happy Birthday"
- **Title Row 1:** "Sarah"
- **Title Row 2:** "Miller"
- **End Text:** "Don't Get Stranger"

### Tribute style
- **Start Text:** "In Memory Of"
- **Title Row 1:** "Our Friend"
- **Title Row 2:** "The Hero"
- **End Text:** "Never Forgotten"

### Team or group
- **Start Text:** "Presented by"
- **Title Row 1:** "The Basement"
- **Title Row 2:** "Crew"
- **End Text:** "Game Night 2026"

<Note>
  Keep text concise for maximum impact. The Stranger Things aesthetic works best with short, punchy phrases.
</Note>

## Tips for personalization

### Make it memorable

The Stranger Things intro is iconic because of its simplicity and atmosphere. Keep your text short and impactful to maintain that same powerful effect.

### Choose the right resolution

- **SD:** Quick to process, perfect for social media stories or quick shares
- **HD:** Great balance for most YouTube videos and general sharing
- **4K:** Best for professional-looking content or if you plan to edit the video further

### Experiment with different styles

Since you can create multiple videos, try different approaches:
- Serious and dramatic
- Funny and playful  
- Mysterious and cryptic
- Personal and heartfelt

### Think about your audience

Consider who will see your video:
- Fellow fans will appreciate deep-cut references
- General audiences respond better to clear, accessible text
- Personal recipients will love inside jokes or meaningful phrases
